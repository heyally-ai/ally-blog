Themes for Ally!

Now you can customize the look and feel of Ally on iOS and macOS with three themes.

Newspaper theme
Newspaper is a grayscale monochrome theme for people who like light themes. It has a near white background, containers and fields are shades of gray, and text is near black gray.

Monochrome theme
For dark theme lovers, monochrome has a near black background, dark container backgrounds, and near white text.

Primary theme
My personal favorite, Primary is a low contrast theme with a brown background, green highlights, a text is shades of cream-colored off whites. The theme for those who like a bit of color.
