AI-enabled PKMs in 2026

When AI burst into public awareness in 2023, intrepid developers immediately saw the potential to apply AI to something it’s really good at: managing text.

Notion

Notion is a mature app with a lot of powerful features, which for some can also be its downside. For many, it’s distracting and they end up spending more time building the perfect “Notion productivity system” rather than actually getting things done.

Notion’s AI features are fairly typical of pre-existing apps that later added AI capabilities — they’re tacked on instead of deeply integrated into the PKM workflow.

Notion is not a local solution which means you’ll be sharing your data with Notion and whatever LLM providers it uses.

Ally

Ally is unique because it’s a true “AI-first” app that was designed from the ground up for advanced AI-PKM capabilities. While you’re working, a lot is happening in the background.

Perhaps Ally’s standout feature is its unique “Shared Context” feature which means that the LLM always knows what you’re working on — no need to copy and paste or give it context. It sees exactly what you see.
